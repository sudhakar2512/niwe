Introduction

National Institute of Wind Energy (NIWE) as a Third Party Technical Inspection Body (Type- A )endeavour to provide Quality Services on the components used in the Renewable Energy Industry (On-shore and Off -shore). NIWE has a quality system that complies with the international standards ISO/IEC 17020:2012.

NIWE has developed and documented the general, structural, resource and procedural requirements for Type - A inspection body as per ISO/IEC 17020:2012 compliance and to assure quality service to the customers.

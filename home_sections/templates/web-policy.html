Content Review Policy

This content Review Policy has been formulated to keep the content on the NIWE website current and up- to-date. Since the type of the content on the NIWE website varies, different Review timelines are defined for the diverse content elements. This Review Policy is based on different types of content elements, their validity and relevance as well as the archival policy.
As a general rule:
The website content is reviewed every quarterly to provide up to date information to users.

Website Monitoring Plan

The NIWE website is monitored periodically to address and fix the quality and compatibility issues of the website on the following parameters:

  • Functionality: All modules of the website are regularly tested for their functionality. It is ensured that interactive components of the site are working smoothly.
  • Performance: Improvements are made continuously to increase the site performance.
  • Spelling errors: The errors are rectified as soon as they are reported.
  • Feedback: Feedback from the visitors is the best way to judge a website's performance and make necessary improvements. A proper mechanism for feedback has implemented.

Cookies

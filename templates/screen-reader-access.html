Screen Reader Access

The National Institute Of Wind Energy website complies with Guidelines for Indian Government Websites and World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0 level A. This will enable people with visual impairments access the website using technologies, such as screen readers. The information of the website is accessible with different screen readers, such as JAWS, NVDA, SAFA, Supernova and Window-Eyes.

Some Screen Readers Are As Follows

Screen Access For All (SAFA) http://safa-reader.software.informer.com/download/ Free

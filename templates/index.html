R&D supports time bound and mission oriented Research & Development programmes to achieve world class, reliable and cost effective technology in wind power systems. RDAF supports the industry to analyse the big data obtained from wind and solar resource measurements. view more...

The Division carries out Type certification of wind turbines based on IS/IEC 61400-22, And also involved in creating the necessary IT infrastructure for effective dissemination of information. view more...

The Division provides various services for the healthy and orderly growth of Wind Energy Sector such as: Standardization works in Wind Energy Sector, Facilitation for Grid Synchronization of Prototype of wind turbines and provides the technical support to MNRE for the implementation of Approved List of Models and Manufacturers (Wind) of wind turbines (i.e. ALMM (Wind)) view more..

This Division carries out Wind Turbine Testing as per the National Lab policy of MNRE. view more..

The Division identifies resource rich regions in the country by conducting wind resource micro survey and offers its services to the wind farm developers. To prepare wind map for the nation, it is assessing and analyzing wind resources.view more..

As per the National Offshore Wind Energy Policy 2015, National Institute of Wind Energy is the nodal agency for offshore wind energy development in the country. NIWE is responsible for demarcation of offshore wind blocks based on offshore studies and surveys. NIWE also facilitates obtaining clearances from the Ministries/ departments for the development of offshore wind energy blocks. view more..

To serve as finest information centre in wind energy by collecting, collating and analyzing the related information. National and International training workshops are being organized regularly for the benefit of stakeholders. view more..
